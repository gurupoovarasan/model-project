Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, is a Portuguese football superstar widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history. Known for his exceptional skill, speed, and goal-scoring ability, he has played for top clubs including Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. Ronaldo has won multiple Ballon d'Or awards and numerous league titles, along with leading Portugal to victory in the 2016 UEFA European Championship. Off the pitch, he is known for his philanthropy and business ventures. Ronaldo's dedication, work ethic, and charisma have made him a global icon in sports and popular culture.
